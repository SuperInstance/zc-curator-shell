- `shell` — Bootstrapping/capture algorithms (the-shell)

- `tool` — Utilities, scripts, CLI tools
- `docs` — Documentation, specs, architecture
- `archive` — Inactive, deprecated, or legacy projects
- `stub` — Empty, placeholder, or just‑started repos

**Tags (additional context):**
- `training` — Involves model training or LoRA export
- `edge` — Jetson/edge‑deployment related
- `cloud` — Cloud‑native or lighthouse services
- `rust` / `python` / `cpp` — Primary language
- `experimental` — High‑risk, unproven
- `integrated` — Part of a working fleet system
- `abandoned` — No recent activity, likely dead
