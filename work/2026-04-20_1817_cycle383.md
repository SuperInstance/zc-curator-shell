## 1. Fleet Taxonomy (Derived from Active Fleet Repos)

From reading fleet-knowledge/ and active fleet repos, I derive this working taxonomy:

**Core Domains:**
- **plato** – Training room infrastructure (plato-torch, plato-ensign)
- **holodeck** – Simulation environments (holodeck-rust)
- **fleet-simulator** – Multi-agent simulation
- **constraint-theory** – Geometric/relational reasoning (constraint-theory-core)
- **cudaclaw** – GPU-resident agent runtime
- **flux-runtime** – Self-assembling bytecode VM
- **shell** – Bootstrapping/capture algorithms
- **deadband** – Protocol implementation (deadband-protocol)
- **tools** – Developer utilities, APIs, configs
- **research** – Exploratory code, experiments, rabbit trails
- **docs** – Documentation, memory, brand assets
- **legacy** – Archived, deprecated, or prototype work

**Status Tags:**
- **active** – Actively developed, part of current fleet
- **stale** – Not recently updated but potentially useful
- **archive** – Historical, not for current use
- **seed** – New idea, minimal implementation
