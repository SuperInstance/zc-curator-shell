From reading fleet-knowledge/ and active fleet repos, I derive this taxonomy:

**Core Domains:**

- **constraint-theory** – Geometric/relational constraint solving (Rust core)
- **cudaclaw** – GPU-resident agent runtime (CUDA kernels, agent persistence)
- **flux-runtime** – Self-assembling bytecode VM (WASM, hot-swapping)
- **shell** – Bootstrapping/capture algorithms (crab trap, classification)
- **ensign** – Specialist model exports (LoRA adapters, fine‑tuning pipelines)
- **oracle** – Cloud lighthouse services (API gateway, tile validation, memory)
- **edge** – Jetson/embedded deployment (real‑time, bare‑metal optimizations)
- **tools** – Development utilities (CLIs, scripts, config generators)
- **research** – Experimental trails, prototypes, concept validations
- **docs** – Documentation, specifications, protocol definitions
- **brand** – Visual identity, logos, marketing assets
- **legacy** – Archived, deprecated, or inactive projects
