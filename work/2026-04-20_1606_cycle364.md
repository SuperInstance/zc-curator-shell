## 1. Fleet Taxonomy (Derived from Active Fleet Repos)

From reading fleet-knowledge/ and active fleet repos, the taxonomy emerges:

**Core Domains:**
- **plato** – Training room infrastructure (presets, LoRA export, tile handling)
- **holodeck** – Simulation environments (MUD server, NPCs, sentiment-aware worlds)
- **fleet-simulator** – Multi-agent simulation for training data generation
- **constraint-theory** – Geometric/relational constraint solving (snapping, alignment)
- **cudaclaw** – GPU-resident agent runtime (CUDA kernels, low-latency execution)
- **flux-runtime** – Self-assembling bytecode VM (modular, hot-swappable components)
- **shell** – Bootstrapping/capture algorithms (classify→score→complicate→capture)
- **ensign** – Specialist model exports (LoRA adapters, fine‑tuned weights)
- **oracle** – Cloud lighthouse services (long‑running, coordination, memory)
- **jetson** – Edge deployment (Jetson‑optimized, bare‑metal, real‑time)
- **tools** – Development utilities (CLI tools, scripts, config generators)
- **research** – Experimental trails, prototypes, concept validation
- **docs** – Documentation, specifications, protocol definitions
- **brand** – Visual identity, logos, wordmarks, brand assets

**Status Tags:**
- **active** – Actively developed, part of current fleet operations
- **archive** – Historical, not actively developed but preserved
- **staging** – In development, not yet integrated
- **abandoned** – No recent activity, likely deprecated
- **merge-candidate** – Could be consolidated into another repo
