- `documentation` — Pure docs, wikis, notes
- `asset` — Brand assets, images, design files
- `learning` — Tutorials, examples, educational content

**Status Tags:**
- `active` — Actively maintained, recent commits
- `stale` — No recent activity but potentially useful
- `abandoned` — No activity >1 year, likely dead
- `seed` — Minimal starter, needs development
