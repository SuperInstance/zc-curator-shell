From reading fleet-knowledge/ and active fleet repos, I observe the following taxonomy in use:

- **fleet‑simulator** – Fleet‑level simulation for generating training data.

- **deadband‑protocol** – Implementation of the P0/P1/P2 decision protocol.
- **agent‑runtime** – Core agent execution environment, message‑passing, state management.
- **training‑tiles** – Tile format, validation, submission pipelines.
- **docs‑and‑memory** – Documentation, memory files, brand assets, operational notes.
- **research‑trails** – Exploratory research, market analysis, concept prototypes.
- **tooling‑and‑ops** – Build scripts, service guards, deployment, monitoring.
- **experimental** – One‑off experiments, quick tests, sandbox projects.
- **archive‑inactive** – Deprecated, superseded, or abandoned work.
