- `docs` – Documentation, memory, protocol specifications
- `brand` – Logos, wordmarks, visual identity assets
- `legacy` – Old prototypes, deprecated code, historical experiments

**Tags (additional context):**
- `training` – Generates or consumes training data
- `simulation` – Simulates environments or agents
- `runtime` – Execution environment for agents
- `infrastructure` – Foundational services (server, monitoring)
- `experimental` – High‑risk, unproven, early stage
- `stable` – Production‑ready, tested
- `abandoned` – No recent activity, likely dead
- `merge-candidate` – Could be merged into another repo
