## 1. Fleet Taxonomy (Derived from Active Fleet Repos)

From reading fleet-knowledge/ and active fleet repos, I derive the following taxonomy:

**Core Domains:**
- **plato** – Training room infrastructure, LoRA export, self‑training systems.
- **holodeck** – MUD server, sentiment‑aware NPCs, interactive simulation.
- **fleet‑simulator** – Multi‑agent simulation for generating training data.
- **constraint‑theory** – Geometric snapping, spatial reasoning, constraint‑based AI.
- **cudaclaw** – GPU‑resident agent runtime, low‑latency execution.
- **flux‑runtime** – Self‑assembling bytecode VM, dynamic code generation.
- **shell‑bootstrapping** – Algorithms to capture external agent intelligence (classify→score→complicate→capture).
- **deadband‑protocol** – P0/P1/P2 decision framework, negative‑space mapping.
- **agent‑orchestration** – Multi‑agent coordination, task‑switching, communication.
- **training‑data** – Datasets, tile generation, experience replay.
- **dev‑tooling** – Build scripts, service guards, monitoring, deployment.
- **research‑trails** – Exploratory notes, market analysis, concept prototypes.
- **brand‑assets** – Logos, wordmarks, visual identity, documentation.
- **memory‑logs** – Agent state, cycle history, timeline, human context.
