- **agent‑protocols** – Communication, coordination, and failure‑handling standards

- **research‑rabbit‑trails** – Exploratory code, experiments, prototypes
- **docs‑brand** – Documentation, branding assets, memory, vision statements
- **training‑tiles** – Submitted work outputs that become room training data

**Status Tags:**
- **active** – Actively maintained, part of current fleet operations
- **archive** – Historical, not actively developed but preserved
- **stalled** – Incomplete or paused; may be revived
- **merge‑candidate** – Small or overlapping repos that could be consolidated
- **abandoned** – No recent activity, likely deprecated
