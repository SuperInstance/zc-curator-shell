- **docs‑tools** – Documentation generators, tooling, scripts.
- **research‑trails** – Exploratory code, experiments, rabbit‑hole investigations.
- **legacy‑archive** – Old prototypes, deprecated systems, historical artifacts.

**Tags (for cross‑cutting concerns):**
- `rust` | `python` | `cpp` – Primary language.
- `training` – Produces or consumes training data.
- `simulation` – Simulates environments or agents.
- `runtime` – Execution environment for agents.
- `protocol` – Defines communication or decision standards.
- `tool` – Utility, script, or helper.
- `experiment` – Not yet integrated; high risk.
- `abandoned` – No recent activity; candidate for archival.
