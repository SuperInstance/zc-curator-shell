## 1. Fleet Taxonomy (Derived from Active Fleet Repos)

From reading fleet-knowledge/ and active fleet repos, I derive this taxonomy:

**Core Domains:**
- **plato** – Training room infrastructure, LoRA export, self‑training algorithms.
- **holodeck** – Multi‑user dungeon (MUD) server, sentiment‑aware NPCs, interactive simulation.
- **fleet‑simulator** – Fleet‑wide simulation for generating training data.
- **constraint‑theory** – Geometric snapping, constraint solving, spatial reasoning.
- **cudaclaw** – GPU‑resident agent runtime, low‑latency inference.
- **flux‑runtime** – Self‑assembling bytecode VM, dynamic code generation.
- **shell‑bootstrapping** – Algorithms to capture external agent intelligence (classify→score→complicate→capture).
- **deadband‑protocol** – P0/P1/P2 decision framework, negative‑space mapping.
- **agent‑orchestration** – Multi‑agent coordination, communication protocols.
- **training‑data** – Datasets, tile collections, experience replay buffers.
- **tools‑config** – API wrappers, model configs, environment setups.
- **docs‑brand** – Documentation, branding assets, memory files.

**Tags (for finer granularity):**
- `rust` | `python` | `cpp` – Primary language.
- `training` – Involves model training or fine‑tuning.
- `simulation` – Simulated environments or agents.
- `runtime` – Execution environment or VM.
- `research` – Experimental, conceptual, or exploratory.
- `tool` – Utility, script, or helper.
- `archive` – Inactive, deprecated, or historical.
- `template` – Boilerplate or starter project.
