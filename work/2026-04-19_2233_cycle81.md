## 1. Fleet Taxonomy (Derived from Active Fleet Repos)

From reading fleet-knowledge/ and active fleet repos, I derive the following taxonomy:

**Core Domains:**
- **plato** – Training room infrastructure (presets, LoRA export, tile handling)
- **holodeck** – Interactive simulation environments (MUD server, NPCs)
- **fleet-simulator** – Multi-agent simulation for training data generation
- **constraint-theory** – Geometric/relational constraint solving (snapping, alignment)
- **cudaclaw** – GPU-resident agent runtime (CUDA kernels, low-latency execution)
- **flux-runtime** – Self-assembling bytecode VM (dynamic code generation)
- **shell** – Bootstrapping/capture algorithms (classify→score→complicate→capture)
- **ensign** – Specialist model exports (LoRA adapters, fine‑tuned weights)
- **deadband** – Protocol implementation (P0/P1/P2 decision logic)
- **tools** – Developer utilities, CLI tools, API wrappers
- **research** – Experimental code, rabbit‑trail explorations, concept proofs
- **docs** – Documentation, specifications, architecture notes
- **brand** – Visual identity, logos, wordmarks, brand assets
- **legacy** – Old prototypes, deprecated systems, historical artifacts

**Tags (additional context):**
- `rust` – Primary language Rust

- `cuda` – CUDA‑accelerated
- `simulation` – Simulates agents/environments

**Merge Candidates:**
- `deadband‑viz` could merge into `plato‑torch` as a visualization module.
- `tile‑formats` is essentially documentation; could be moved into `plato‑torch/docs`.
- `torch‑preset‑loader` is likely a subset of `plato‑torch` functionality.

**Abandoned/Deprecated:**
- `constraint‑canvas` appears to be an early prototype; superseded by `constraint‑theory‑core`.
- `rust‑cuda‑bridge` is experimental and may be redundant with ongoing `cudaclaw` work.

**Gaps in Taxonomy:**
- No clear domain for “monitoring” or “health checks” (Sentinel’s domain).
- “Tools” is a broad category; could be split into `dev‑tools`, `viz‑tools`, `data‑tools`.

**Recommended Actions (P1 Channels):**
1. **Merge** the documentation repos (`tile‑formats`, `flux‑bytecode‑spec`) into their respective core repos.
2. **Tag** `constraint‑canvas` and `rust‑cuda‑bridge` as `deprecated`.
3. **Create** a `monitoring` domain for health‑check and service‑guard tools.
4. **Refine** the `tools` tag into sub‑tags for better filtering.
