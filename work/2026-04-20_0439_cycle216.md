**Support Categories:**
- `docs` – Documentation, specifications, protocol definitions
- `tools` – Utilities, scripts, CLI tools for fleet operations
- `research` – Exploratory code, experiments, rabbit‑trail investigations
- `archive` – Historical, deprecated, or inactive projects
- `template` – Boilerplate, starter kits, cookiecutters
- `demo` – Visual demonstrations, example implementations
- `test` – Test suites, benchmarking, validation harnesses

**Lifecycle Tags:**
- `active` – Actively maintained, part of current fleet operations
- `stale` – Low activity, may need review or revival
- `abandoned` – No recent commits, likely orphaned
- `merge‑candidate` – Overlaps with another repo; consider consolidation
