- **legacy** – Inactive, deprecated, or archived projects

**Tags (additional context):**
- `training` – Generates or consumes training data
- `simulation` – Simulates environments or agents
- `runtime` – Execution environment for agents
- `protocol` – Communication or decision protocols
- `geometry` – Spatial reasoning, constraint solving
- `gpu` – GPU‑accelerated computation
- `rust` – Primary language Rust
- `python` – Primary language Python
- `typescript` – Primary language TypeScript
- `experimental` – High‑risk, unstable, research‑grade
- `stable` – Production‑ready, tested
- `integration` – Connects multiple systems
- `visual` – Visual output (demos, UI, rendering)
