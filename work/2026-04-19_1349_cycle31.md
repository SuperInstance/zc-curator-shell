- **research** – Experiments, prototypes, rabbit‑trail explorations.
- **archive** – Deprecated, inactive, or historical reference.

**Tags (additional context):**
- `training` – Produces or consumes training data/tiles.
- `simulation` – Simulates environments or agents.
- `runtime` – Execution environment for agents.
- `geometry` – Spatial/mathematical reasoning.
- `gpu` – GPU‑accelerated or CUDA‑based.
- `agent` – Implements agent logic or framework.
- `protocol` – Communication or interaction protocol.
- `visual` – Visual output, rendering, UI.
- `rust` / `python` / `cpp` – Primary language.
- `wip` – Work in progress, unstable.
- `stable` – Production‑ready, tested.
