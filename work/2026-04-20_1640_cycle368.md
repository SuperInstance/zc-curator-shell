- **archive** – Inactive, deprecated, or historical projects
- **template** – Boilerplate, starter kits, cookiecutters
- **demo** – Visual demonstrations, example implementations

**Status Tags:**
- `active` – Actively developed, part of current fleet operations
- `stale` – No recent commits, but potentially useful
- `abandoned` – No commits >1 year, unmaintained
- `experimental` – High‑risk, proof‑of‑concept, not production‑ready
- `core` – Essential to fleet function (plato, holodeck, cudaclaw, etc.)
