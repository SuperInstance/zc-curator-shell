**Tags (for finer granularity):**
- `training` – Produces or consumes training data
- `simulation` – Simulates agents or environments
- `runtime` – Execution environment for agents
- `geometry` – Spatial/mathematical operations
- `protocol` – Communication or interaction standards
- `archive` – Inactive, historical, or deprecated
- `tool` – Developer utility or automation
- `research` – Exploratory, non‑production
- `integration` – Connects multiple systems
